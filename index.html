About the Trip

Join a bunch of dads and sons (and grandads) for an epic camping weekend in Joshua Tree National Park. Enjoy nights under the endless stars and days adventuring with your boys and other men. Come Thursday night or whenever you can make it Friday or Saturday.

Registration cost includes the cost of the campsites, activities, and all meals from Friday morning through Sunday Morning. Activities include top rope rock climbing, an epic zipline, hiking, star gazing, and lots of good time with good people.

Once you've registered, you'll receive a Venmo/Zelle request to pay which will officially secure your spot!
